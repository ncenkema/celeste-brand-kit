# Messaging

## Pillars

1. **Your skin is not the same throughout your cycle.** The core insight the whole brand stands on.
2. **A platform and a product line, built as one system.** Not tracking alone, not a bottle alone.
3. **Warm, intelligent, specific.** Never vague wellness, never clinical sterility.

## Approved taglines

- "skincare made to cycle with you." (primary — used on billboard, A6)
- "built to cycle with you." (secondary — used in brand seed, A1)

## Key messages by product/phase

| Product | Phase | Core message |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Mend (01) | Menstrual | Built for the days your skin needs the most care. |
| Float (02) | Follicular | Built for the days your skin bounces back. |
| Opal (03) | Ovulatory | Built for the days your skin is at its brightest. |
| Lull (04) | Luteal | Built for the days your skin needs steadying. |

## Boilerplate

Celeste is a skin health platform and product line built entirely around the hormonal cycle. Four
phases, one personalized protocol — skincare that moves with the body, not against it.

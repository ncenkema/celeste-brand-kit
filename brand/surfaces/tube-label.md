# Surface: Tube Label

## What appears

- **Technical text** — DM Mono, top of tube face. Two lines: product number and name, then phase
  name. E.g. "03 - OPAL" / "OVULATORY PHASE."
- **Wordmark** — "Celeste" in cursive script (Cedarville Cursive), centered, running vertically with the
  tube's form.
- **Phase indicator** — four small circles near the base; one filled marks the product's position
  in the cycle.

## Register

Minimal, technical, no marketing copy. This is identification, not persuasion — the tube states
what it is and where it sits in the cycle, nothing more.

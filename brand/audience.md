# Audience

## Primary

Women 25–40 who care about: their health, skincare, clean ingredients, solutions that understand
them, simplicity, cycle-tracking.

From the cycle-tracking obsessive to the woman who just noticed her skin changes every month and
finally wants solutions.

## What they're tired of

- Routines built for a fixed "skin type" that ignore weekly change.
- Wellness platforms with no real product behind them.
- Products with no intelligence behind them — one-size-fits-all in a bottle.
- Generic, undifferentiated wellness messaging that doesn't say anything specific.

## What they want instead

- A routine that moves with them, phase by phase.
- Language that's plain, direct, and specific — not performed or clinical.
- A brand with the intimacy of the friend who texts you what phase she's in.

## Future expansion audiences

Celeste Natal (prenatal), Celeste Postpartum, Celeste Peri (perimenopause) — same core belief
(skin changes with hormonal life stage), applied to adjacent life stages beyond the monthly cycle.

# Values

Operating principles — how Celeste shows up, every time.

- **When choosing between clinical and warm, choose warm — then make it precise.** Softness without
  rigor reads as vague. Precision without warmth reads as sterile. Celeste holds both.
- **Specificity over vagueness.** Say the phase, the product, the reason. Never "balance" or "glow"
  with nothing behind it.
- **Warm, not soft.** Editorial and sensory, never soft-focus or precious.
- **One source of truth.** Every touchpoint pulls from the same brand, so nothing drifts or
  contradicts itself.
- **Confident, not showy.** Celeste knows what it's doing and doesn't need to prove it. Say the
  thing plainly and let it land — no over-explaining, no flexing.
- **Realistic and representative.** Real skin, real texture, real hands — not an idealized or
  narrow standard of who this is for.
